ASSIGNMENT 7 - Weather by Israel Kamakawiwo'ole

Designed with novelty (as opposed to practicality) in mind, Weather by Isreal Kamakawiwo'ole is a climatic reporting application that provides the user with information as framed by famous Hawaiian musician and activist Isreal Kamakawiwo'ole. The application's home page provides the user with temperature information for Honolulu, Hawaii (Isreal's hometown). Users can then search for any city and Israel will inform them of the temperature in that location relative to Honolulu.

This application also conveys temperature information through a non-visual output - sound. Depending on the temperature difference betweened the searched city and Honolulu, the tempo of Israel's "Over the Rainbow" will speed up (for locations hotter than Honolulu) or slow down (for locations colder Honolulu). The greater the temperature difference the more noticable the tempo change in the song will be.

Suggested cities for a fast tempo: Khartoum, Santiago, and Bamako. Suggested cities for a slow tempo include: Moscow and Norilsk (really slow, but so good!!).
