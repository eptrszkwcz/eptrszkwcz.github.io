This visulation utilizes a geojson file created from the Manhattan Pluto dataset. After loading the json data, I established the x and y values, as well as the lot geometry, for the scatterplot by iterating through the json. In order to create the lot geometry, I then iterated through the coordinates of the verticies of each shape. I added or subtracted a unique x constrant and unique y constant from every coordinate within a given lot shape in order to transform the location of that lot from its position within the map to its position within the scatterplot.

These processes were relatively complex (at least to me!); if I had more time there are numerous things I would include/improve for this graphic. Due to the many transformations the datums (x,y axes) at some point became off relative to where i expected them to be. This did not allow me to easily plot the scale axes for built and potential building area. Because all of the vacant lots fall on the x-axis they are lost somewhere in cyber world as well. I would really like to fix this.

Ideally, when the mouse hovers over a given polygon, I would like it to highlight (with black stroke) and I would like the address to pop up (which I could retrieve from the json).
